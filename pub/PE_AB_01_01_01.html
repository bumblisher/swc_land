Greeting

KOREAN ACCREDITATION BOARD
OF NURSING EDUCATION

Hello and welcome to the website of
Korean Accreditation Board of Nursing Education
(KABONE).

Greetings and warm welcome to all of you visiting KABONE Website.

Incorporated as a foundation in 2004, the Korean Accreditation Board of Nursing (KABONE) has been dedicated to providing a variety
of services for professional nurses and nurse hopefuls, including accreditation of nursing education, designation review of four-year
course nursing department, designation of educational institution for advanced nurse practitioner(ANP), qualification examination
management of ANP, designation and evaluation of education and training institution for assistant nurses, etc.

KABONE will contribute to producing ANP with top notch nursing service capabilities and global competitiveness as our society
demands by ensuring efficient and effective qualification examination management of ANP and quality management of educational
institution for ANP etc.

As the President, reaffirm my commitment to enabling KABONE to provide such services in an efficient and effective manner.

We appreciate your interests in our services and look forward to you finding and sharing information and insight in our Website as a forum of ideas.

Sincerely
