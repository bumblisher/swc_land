Purpose of KABONE Foundation

The KABONE institute was established in 2003 and is the only institution in Korea for the accreditation of nursing education recognized by Ministry of Education. KABONE is striving to foster competent nurses as required by society while developing the nursing education.

  • 1. Accreditation of nursing education
  • 2. Qualification examination management of advanced nurse practitioner(ANP)
  • 3. Projects entrusted by the government

    - Designation of educational institution for ANP

    - Designation review of four-year course nursing department

    - Designation and evaluation of education and training institution for assistant nurses

  • 4. Research and development related to the nursing education
